About

It’s time to debut the new cart!!! We will be bringing it down to Winterfest to warm you up with some delicious homemade goods! We will be serving 3 flavors of ravioli, a vegan and gluten free tomato basil soup, and 2 different flavors of fried ravioli!! Come say hi this Friday-Sunday and check out all the action!


Vegan and gluten free menu options are available. Call, Email, or follow us on Instagram and Facebook for more information!
